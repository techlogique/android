Types of cookies Service Provider may use:


Strictly necessary cookies: these are cookies required for the operation of a website and App, such as enabling you to log into secure areas.


Performance cookies: these types of cookies recognize and count the number of visitors to a website and users of an App and see how users move around in each. This information is used to improve the way the website and App work.


Functionality cookies: these cookies recognize when you return to a website or App, enable personalized content and recognize and remember your preferences.


Targeting cookies: These cookies record your visit to a website or App, including the individual pages visited and the links followed.


Generally, the strictly necessary cookies and some performance and functionality cookies only last for expiring when you close an App; the functionality cookies and some targeting and performance cookies may last for a longer period of time.
